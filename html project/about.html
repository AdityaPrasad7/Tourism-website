About Us

Bhubaneswar, the heart and soul of Odisha, is a city that breathes history and exhales culture. Nestled in the eastern part of India, this city is not just a destination; it's an experience waiting to be embraced. With roots dating back to ancient times, Bhubaneswar is a living testament to the country's rich heritage.

At Bhubaneswar Tourism, we are more than just guides; we are storytellers, weaving the tale of this city's glorious past and vibrant present. Our journey through Bhubaneswar takes you beyond the tourist spots; it immerses you in the very essence of the city.

Picture yourself wandering through the narrow lanes of Old Town, where every cobblestone has a story to tell. Imagine standing before the Lingaraja Temple, a masterpiece of Kalinga architecture, feeling the spiritual aura that has enchanted pilgrims for centuries. Taste the flavors of Odia cuisine, where spices dance on your palate, and savor the legendary Pahala Rasgulla, a sweet indulgence that captures the essence of the state.

But Bhubaneswar is not just about its historical grandeur; it's a thriving modern metropolis. The city resonates with the laughter of students from its prestigious educational institutions and the hum of technology from its burgeoning IT sector. It embraces the future while honoring its roots.

Our tours are crafted with passion, designed to make you fall in love with Bhubaneswar. Explore the ancient caves of Udayagiri and Khandagiri, where Jain monks once sought enlightenment. Discover the artistic brilliance at the Odisha State Museum, where every artifact narrates a chapter of Odisha's legacy.

But Bhubaneswar is not just about the places; it's about the people. Meet the artisans who create intricate Pattachitra paintings, and learn the age-old techniques passed down through generations. Engage with the locals at Ekamra Haat, a bustling crafts village, and witness the diversity of Odisha's handicrafts.

Our team of knowledgeable guides is not just versed in facts; they are keepers of folklore, custodians of tradition, and enthusiasts eager to share the city's best-kept secrets. We promise you more than a tour; we promise you an odyssey.

Come, join us on this expedition through time and tradition. Let the ancient sculptures and modern skyscrapers of Bhubaneswar inspire you. Let the scent of incense and the rhythm of traditional music awaken your senses. Let the warmth of the people and the richness of the culture leave an indelible mark on your heart.

Bhubaneswar is not just a city; it's an emotion. And we, at Bhubaneswar Tourism, invite you to feel it.
